# User Stories for Judy (Financial Director)

## Persona: Judy
**Role:** Financial Director  
**Background:** Judy is responsible for the company's treasury and financial health. She has a deep background in traditional finance, ROI analysis, and tax compliance but finds technical blockchain jargon (like "cumulative factors" or "staking rounds") confusing.  
**Objective:** To manage the company's Livepeer (LPT) position as an investment asset with the same rigor and clarity as a traditional brokerage account.

---

## Why the Current "Explorer" Doesn't Work for Judy
Judy currently avoids the standard Livepeer Explorer because it feels like a "tool for developers" rather than a dashboard for investors.

| Her Problem | Why Explorer Fails Her Today |
|:---|:---|
| **"Where is the spreadsheet?"** | There is no way to export data. She has to copy-paste numbers into Excel for her monthly reports. |
| **"Am I winning or losing?"** | She can't see her total ROI across all wallets in one place. |
| **"Is this manager reliable?"** | Performance data is technical and doesn't explicitly flag when a manager misses a payment in "plain English." |
| **"What about taxes?"** | No transaction history for accounting. She has to dig through Etherscan, which is a nightmare. |
| **"Real money value?"** | Everything is listed in LPT tokens. Judy needs to know the value in **USD** to understand the actual impact on the balance sheet. |

---

## MyWallet: Business-Centric User Stories (P1 & P2 Focus)

### P1 — High Impact (The "Must-Haves" for Finance)

| # | As a Financial Director, I want to... | So that I can... |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **S7** | **Export an Automated Audit Trail** with date-stamped transactions and gas costs. | Hand a clean CSV to my accountant for tax filings without manual guesswork. |
| **S8** | **Run a "What-If" Rebalancing Simulator** that calculates the cost of the 7-day waiting period. | Decide if moving funds to a higher-yield manager is actually worth the temporary loss of earnings. |
| **S9** | **See a "Reliability Score"** for my managers (How often did they actually pay out?). | Identify and fire unreliable managers before they impact our quarterly returns. |
| **S10** | **Compare my ROI against the Market Average.** | Report to the board whether our staking strategy is outperforming the rest of the network. |
| **S11** | **Manage everything from one dashboard** (Bonding/Claiming). | Stop switching between multiple complex websites just to move our assets. |
| **S12** | **See "Cash-Out" Countdown Timers** for unbonding funds. | Plan our company liquidity and know exactly when cash will be available. |

### P2 — Medium Impact (The "Professional Edge")

| # | As a Financial Director, I want to... | So that I can... |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **S13** | **Generate a One-Click P&L Report** (Profit & Loss) for the entire portfolio. | Have a professional financial statement ready for our internal audits at any moment. |
| **S14** | **See a USD Value Overlay** on all my token balances and returns. | Understand our true financial exposure and ROI in the currency we use to pay our bills. |
| **S15** | **Maintain an "Investment Watchlist"** of potential new managers. | Monitor candidates over time and "pre-vet" them before we commit large amounts of capital. |
| **S16** | **Receive an "Alert" on Risk Changes** (e.g., if a manager suddenly changes their commission). | Act instantly to protect our capital from unexpected fee Hikes. |
| **S17** | **Automate the Compounding of Rewards.** | Ensure we are maximizing our ROI without me having to manually log in every few days. |
| **S18** | **See the Voting Record of our managers.** | Ensure the people managing our funds share our company's long-term values on protocol governance. |

---

## Summary of Value Add for Judy
Moving from the **Explorer** to **MyWallet** changes Judy's experience:
* **From:** "Struggling with blockchain data" **To:** "Managing a financial asset."
* **From:** "Manual data entry" **To:** "Automated financial reporting."
* **From:** "Guessing at ROI" **To:** "Data-driven investment decisions."
